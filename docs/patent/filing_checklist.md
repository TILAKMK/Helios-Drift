# PATENT PROVISIONAL FILING OPERATIONAL CHECKLIST

This checklist guides the legal filing process for the Phantom Protocol provisional patent application.

---

## 1. Documentation Preparation
- [ ] **Application Cover Sheet (PTO/SB/16)**: Fill out inventor details (Tilak M K) and title.
- [ ] **Written Specification**: Formatted markdown/PDF from `docs/patent/provisional_application.md`.
- [ ] **Claims Section**: Detailed list of 10 structural claims mapping to codebase implementations.
- [ ] **Drawings Sheet**: Exported TikZ architectural layouts and system sequence diagrams.
- [ ] **Fee Disclosure**: Review small/micro entity status forms.

---

## 2. USPTO Filing Fees (Standard Schedules)
- **Micro Entity**: ~$60 USD
- **Small Entity**: ~$120 USD
- **Large Entity**: ~$240 USD

---

## 3. Submission Steps (EFS-Web / Patent Center)
1. **Log in** to the USPTO Patent Center portal.
2. **Select** "New Application" -> "Provisional Application".
3. **Upload** the Cover Sheet, Specification PDF, Claims PDF, and Drawings PDF.
4. **Calculate** and pay the filing fees online.
5. **Record** the Application Number and Filing Date on the receipt.
